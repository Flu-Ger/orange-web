Orange is Open Source

Orange is open source and free software. You are free to use it and you are free to redistribute it and/or modify it under the terms of the GNU General Public License. By being open source it allows you to see how it works, how are its algorithms implemented. It allows you to understand and learn from it. And build upon it new things.

Development

Orange development is not just public, it is open to you, to community. You can browse the source code, open and follow development through tickets, contribute patches or help with documentation. Join!
